In this we learn about commands with arguments. The first word is the command and all the words after that are the arguments. 
In this case `hello` was the command and `hackers` was the argument. Thus, our input to the parser is `hello hackres`.
```
Connected!
hacker@hello~intro-to-arguments:~$ hello hackers
Success! Here is your flag:
pwn.college{0XBRY0BiItJ9PBgXXjJUiNBUKPS.dhjNyUDL2ETO0czW}
```
